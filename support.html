Sparkly - Support

Thank you for using Sparkly! Below you'll find answers to common questions.

FAQ

How do I remove ads?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Remove Ads". This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all ads and supports the developer.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings and select your preferred language (English, Russian, or Polish).

Can I add my own events?

Yes! Tap the "+" button on the Events tab to create custom events with your own name, category, and tags.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your data stays on your device. Sparkly works fully offline and does not upload any data to external servers.
